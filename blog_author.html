About the Author

I’ve been in the Tech space for more than 8 years, helping businesses build and improve their digital and tech presence and assisting with sales pipelines, business development functions, and funnels.
I truly believe that business is about people. That’s why building a great business network is my biggest passion. I love meeting new people, making new connections, learning about other companies, and understanding what drives people to do what they do.
And as my experience shows, a business that grounds on a good, trustful human relationship has much more chances for success. For both sides.

Apart from being a business development professional, I love music, adore dogs, and traveling.
I have a zwergschnauzer Boston, and he’s my true friend and soulmate.
